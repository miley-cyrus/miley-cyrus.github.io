Miley Cyrus releases 'Mother's Daughter', a feminist anthem with a very special protagonist: her mother Tish Cyrus.

Miley Cyrus has done it again. The American singer is on everyone's lips again, and this time it's not about her participation in the series Black Mirror or any of her extravagant outfits, but about her latest music video. It's called Mother's Daughter, and it promises to be not only one of the artist's most outstanding songs, but also a new feminist anthem. Power, freedom and vindication. It has it all.

Today, the singer has been giving us small previews of what awaits us in this new song. Images of the women who starred in the music video, all of whom are known for their activist roles and their constant struggle in society, women whose sole aim is to honour inclusion and diversity. Among them all, one name stands out, Tish Cyrus, Miley's mother, who perfectly represents the title chosen for the song.
